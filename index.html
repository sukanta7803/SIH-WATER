Comprehensive Health Protection

Our integrated platform combines mobile technology, artificial intelligence, and community engagement to create a robust early warning system for water-borne diseases.

Mobile Data Collection

Health workers and community volunteers collect data via mobile apps and SMS, ensuring comprehensive coverage even in remote areas.

AI Outbreak Prediction

Advanced machine learning models analyze symptoms, water quality, and seasonal patterns to predict potential disease outbreaks.

Water Quality Monitoring

Integration with IoT sensors and test kits to continuously monitor water source contamination and safety parameters.

Real-time Alerts

Instant notifications to district health officials and local governance bodies when outbreak risks are detected.

Multilingual Support

Community interface available in local tribal languages with offline functionality for areas with limited connectivity.

Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive visualization tools for health departments to track hotspots, monitor interventions, and allocate resources effectively.
